Scallops

Your scallops can turn out just like this!

Prior to creating this recipe I had always wondered how I could have decadent, rich, delicious scallops in the comfort of my own home, but now overpriced restaurant scallops are a thing of the past!

This recipe will guide you to create the most delicious scallops in a lemon and garlic butter sauce that will leave everyone who tastes them amazed that they were created by you!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sea scallops
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbs unsalted butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • olive oil, if desired

Steps

  1. First, begin by heating a pan over medium-high heat, then add oil
  2. When the oil is shimmering, add scallops to pan and cook on 2 minutes each side, seasoning scallops with salt and pepper
  3. Remove scallops from heat and turn heat to low. Add butter, garlic, lemon juice and season with salt and pepper to taste
  4. When garlic is fragrant and browned slightly, serve over scallops
