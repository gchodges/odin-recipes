Scrambled Eggs

Wonderful breakfast!

These eggs will be deliciously fluffy and perfectly tender!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 Tablespoons Butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a pan over medium, then add butter
  2. Crack eggs directly into pan and scramble
  3. Continuously move the eggs in the pan to form small, fluffy curds
