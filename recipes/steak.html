Pan Seared Steak

Beautifully done steak!

Have you ever attempted to make a delicious, juicy, beautifully crusted steak but your attempts have yielded nothing other than bland grey pieces of tough meat? Today I will teach you how to make the best steak you have ever tasted!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 inch thick cut of steak
  • 3 Tablespoons Butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 sprigs thyme
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • olive oil, if desired

Steps

  1. Season steaks liberally with salt and pepper
  2. Heat a pan over medium-high heat, then add oil
  3. When the oil is shimmering, add steak to the pan. Cook for 3-4 minutes, then flip and cook 3 more minutes for medium-rare
  4. Add butter, crushed garlic, and thyme to the pan. Butter baste the steak for two minutes
  5. let the steak rest for 10 minutes before serving
